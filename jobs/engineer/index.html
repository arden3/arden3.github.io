Architectural Engineer, Project Manager
(Mongolia Office)

Mondreem is an international design and development firm, specialized in high-end residential and commercial projects. Since its inception, Mondreem constantly redefined the impact that design has on the places we live in by building spaces that evoke emotions and enhance the quality of living. Our expertise extends across the areas of development management, architecture, interior design and specialty construction. In addition, we develop branded residences and bespoke furniture for luxury homes and high-end boutiques.



Responsibilities:

  • Planing and directing the engineering of the different design created.
  • Design, calculating and developing engineering solutions
  • Evaluation, development and implementation of complex design and project problem resolutions
  • Oversee projects construction
  • Oversee outsourced products fabrication
  • Oversee technical team works
  • Budgeting, scheduling and planning projects
  • Client contact point during construction process
  • Creation of task/project budgets, work breakdowns, rough order estimates, task prioritization.
  • Provide schedule deadlines and budget constraints for design
  • Prepare reports and other associated project and/or program documentation
  • Research and recommend project management policies, principles, and practices


Requirements:

  • We are looking for an ambitious and motivated personality, outgoing, team-oriented, reliable and resilient, highly organised and efficient, with the capacity to work in a fast-paced environment, both collaboratively and independently, and meet tight deadlines with a positive attitude
  • Successfully completed university studies (bachelor’s degree, or higher) in the field of engineering or related area of study
  • Minimum three years experience in architectural engineering and project management is preferred
  • Advanced skills in CAD and BIM software
  • Capacity to supervise, plan and organize work efficiently
  • Good interpersonal and communication skills
  • Previous experience in a construction company would be a plus
  • Technical knowledge of buildings structure, services (HVAC, water supply, sanitation) and energy (thermal loads, efficiency, etc.)
  • Very good language skills:
    • Mongolian – mother tongue,
    • English – fluent, spoken and written,
    • knowledge of Spanish, Russian, or Arabic would be a plus
  • You should have fun working in a young, creative team in a lively environment


Benefits:

  • Salary to be agreed according to experience
  • Working with a young and multicultural team
  • Offices in the Pyramid Building, at the heart of Ulaanbaatar’s central business district
  • Company retreats
  • Incentive scheme based on peer review


Please send your CV and examples of work to careers@mondreem.com
