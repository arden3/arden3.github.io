Project Blueshift

The Proposal

A Data-Driven Revolution in Frontline Safety

An internal proposal to transform safety culture, prevent incidents, and unlock immense value for Oyu Tolgoi and Rio Tinto.

The Problem We All See

Despite world-class procedures, a gap exists between our documented safety and real-world execution. This gap is created by predictable human factors.

Risk Normalization

On routine tasks, our crews become understandably complacent with familiar hazards. The brain begins to filter out constant risks, leading to potential oversights.

Lack of Personal Accountability

The principle that "safety is everyone's job" can paradoxically become "no one's job" in the critical moment of implementing a specific control.

The Solution: The Blueshift Safety OS

A "Coach on the Shoulder" for every crew. Blueshift is a mobile-first platform that addresses the root causes of safety gaps with two core, interconnected features.

1. AI-Assisted JHA Creation

For new tasks, crews create their JHA in the app. Our AI provides real-time feedback, suggesting missed hazards and stronger controls based on thousands of data points. This turns a compliance task into a powerful learning exercise.

2. Critical Control Assignment

For ALL tasks (JHA or SWP-based), the system requires the assignment of specific, named individuals to each critical control. This transforms shared responsibility into direct, personal accountability.

Immediate Synergy: Making Our Systems Smarter

Blueshift doesn't replace our systems; it elevates them. It's designed to integrate with and enhance our significant investment in the Forwood CRM, turning reactive data into proactive coaching.

The Proactive Feedback Loop

Today: Forwood records a non-compliance after it occurs. This data is valuable but reactive.

With Blueshift: The platform queries Forwood data before a job starts. During a pre-start meeting, the app will flag relevant past issues, allowing crews to proactively prevent repeat incidents.

The Future State: A Predictive Intelligence Engine

As the platform gathers data, it evolves from a system of engagement into a system of intelligence, creating an unbreachable competitive moat and a world-class safety culture.

Hyper-Personalized Coaching

The platform builds a performance profile for each individual, alerting supervisors to provide targeted, positive coaching based on past performance. "Ganbaatar is on an electrical task; his profile shows a minor past struggle with LOTO. This is a perfect coaching opportunity."

Location-Based Risk Profiling

The system creates a "risk heatmap" of the mine. Planners can see that "Sector 7-C has a 40% higher rate of dropped object reports" and can proactively assign additional controls or supervision for tasks in that area.

The Pilot: A Concrete, De-risked Proposal

To prove the value of Blueshift, we propose a six-month pilot with two MCSI crews. The objective is to deliver hard data on its effectiveness.
