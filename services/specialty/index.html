Specialty Construction

Paragraph title

Mondreem specialty construction services delivers luxury residences and commercial projects to the highest standards through innovative construction methods, attention to detail and a commitment to exceptional levels of finishes.

We coordinate not only our own service areas, but also manage all trades, subcontractors and the entire logistics operations. Precise project and construction planning are implemented for fast and efficient project handling, with the ultimate commitment of building with the highest standards wherever the project is located.

Thanks to a dedicated department for the production of bespoke furniture and the installation of exclusive materials and finishes, Mondreem carries out the design and production of all furniture and equipment parts, as well as construction supervision and assembly coordination.

We pride ourselves for being the trusted company to build the exclusive homes of an elite clientele and the boutique builder for renowned brands.

Architecture off

Our main commitment is to design buildings that are attractive, efficient and appropriate to their environment. We believe that the way to create successful architecture is by listening to the clients needs and providing creative and personalised design solutions that fulfil their individual or target market requirements, as well as the location and the environment where each project is located.

Our practice

We practice architecture that evoke feelings, inspire and enhance the quality of the communities where it stands. Mondreem Architectural division operates in the areas of private residential, multi-residential, hospitality, office spaces, commercial building architecture and Master Planning.
