Atelier Mondreem

Bespoke furniture and accessories for the finest homes, luxury boutiques and hotels. Customizable collections, ones-off masterpieces and complete fit-out services.

CRAFTSMANSHIP MADE IN SPAIN

The Atelier Mondreem brings the best Spain has to offer in fine furniture design and craftsmanship, creating bespoke furniture and accessories to complete the finest projects.

LIVING HERITAGE

Each of our pieces is the reflex of the wisdom accumulated from years of experience of the artisans who make them. Through our creations, we will bring you on a journey to discover forgotten arts, luxurious nobel materials and the experience of exclusive fine furniture making.

HAUTE FURNISHING

Atelier Mondreem offers a fully bespoke service using the finest techniques and materials, and crafted with extreme attention to detail and passion by artisans who take great pride on what they do.
