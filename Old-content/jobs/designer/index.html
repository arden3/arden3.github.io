Architectural Graphic Designer
(Mongolia office)

Mondreem is an international design and development firm, specialized in high-end residential and commercial projects. Since its inception, Mondreem constantly redefined the impact that design has on the places we live in by building spaces that evoke emotions and enhance the quality of living. Our expertise extends across the areas of development management, architecture, interior design and specialty construction. In addition, we develop branded residences and bespoke furniture for luxury homes and high-end boutiques.



Responsibilities:

  • Work with an interdisciplinary team on the production of presentations, project proposals, and developing marketing materials that are in line with MONDREEM’s standards and aesthetic
  • Gain a strong understanding of MONDREEM’s portfolio of work, and effectively express the firm’s architectural ideas through branded communications
  • Develop visual content to share through MONDREEM’s company website, and various social media platforms
  • Provide professional graphic design support across the design team
  • Assist architectural teams with graphic and diagrammatic tasks, such as the formatting of project stage reports, design and access statements etc.
  • Participate in the full lifecycle of projects, from design brief to production
  • Think creatively and develop new design concepts, graphics and layouts
  • Contribute to the elevation of quality and mentoring of junior staff
  • Establish, organise and maintain graphic/document templates and resource libraries
  • Brief and liaise with printers and manufacturers
  • Schedule project implementation and define budget implications
  • Design and production of both digital and print PR materials such as invitations, brochures, presentations, company books, publications and competition pitch submissions


Requirements:

  • We are looking for an ambitious and motivated personality, outgoing, team-oriented, reliable and resilient, highly organised and efficient, with the capacity to work in a fast-paced environment, both collaboratively and independently, and meet tight deadlines with a positive attitude
  • Successfully completed university studies (bachelor’s degree, or higher) in the field of graphic design communication, visual design, digital design; or related area of study
  • Minimum three years experience in architecture design, or with a marketing team, is preferred
  • Strong visual and creative skills (sense of space, layout, typography, illustration, visualisation and animation)
  • Advanced skills in the Adobe Creative Suite, in particular, InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop; and Microsoft Office Suite
  • Interest in interdisciplinary, content-based design of sophisticated architecture
  • Understanding of print production processes is advantageous
  • Working knowledge of motion graphics, and film editing and animation would be highly beneficial
  • Very good language skills:
    • Mongolian – mother tongue,
    • English – fluent, spoken and written,
    • knowledge of Spanish, Russian, or Arabic would be a plus
  • You should have fun working in a young, creative team in a lively environment


Benefits:

  • Salary to be agreed according to experience
  • Working with a young and multicultural team
  • Offices in the Pyramid Building, at the heart of Ulaanbaatar’s central business district
  • Company retreats
  • Incentive scheme based on peer review


Please send your CV and examples of work to careers@mondreem.com
